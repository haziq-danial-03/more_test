💼 Professional Journey

I began my professional journey at the age of 18, right after completing my SPM in 2020. Like many students navigating life after school, I took the opportunity to work part-time jobs to gain hands-on experience and build my confidence in the real world.

My first job was at McDonald’s in Bagan Ajam, where I worked as a crew member. It was a fast-paced and demanding environment, but it taught me discipline, punctuality, and how to work efficiently under pressure. I developed a better understanding of customer service and learned how to function as part of a team.

Later, I worked as a sales assistant at Coolblog in Mydin Bukit Mertajam. This role allowed me to polish my interpersonal skills and improve how I communicate with customers. I learned how to handle transactions, operate blending machines, and maintain product quality standards.

These early job experiences shaped my mindset, helped me become more independent, and instilled in me the value of hard work. I believe that working from a young age gave me a strong foundation for my future academic and professional goals.
