About Myself

I’m a dedicated student of Diploma in Library Informatics at UiTM Kedah. I value depth, individuality, and purpose. I’m naturally curious, and I enjoy learning—not just for school or work, but for the sake of understanding the world better.

I'm especially drawn to history, because it gives context to where we are today and offers lessons no textbook can fully contain. I believe that knowing the past helps sharpen how we face the present. I hope someday that I can fulfil my dream of becoming a history teacher or lecturer.

Outside of that, I find joy in music and films, not just as entertainment, but as windows into emotion, culture, and storytelling. Whether it’s a classic film or a song with raw lyrics, I appreciate art that says something real. I also enjoy the challenge of solving a Rubik’s Cube—not just as a test of speed or skill, but as a symbol of patience, pattern, and problem-solving.

It’s a small thing that reflects how I approach bigger things in life: focus, strategy, and persistence. I’m not the type to follow trends blindly. I prefer doing things that make sense to me and reflect who I am. Whether it’s through how I think, speak, or create, I aim to be real, precise, and grounded. I take pride in my identity, and I hold onto values that keep me centered even when things get noisy.
