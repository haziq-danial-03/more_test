🎶 Why I Love Music

Music has always been more than just sound to me — it’s a form of therapy. When the world gets loud or overwhelming, I put on my earphones and everything becomes still. Music helps me sleep better, lifts my mood, and brings clarity to my thoughts.

Whether it’s the calm of an acoustic track, the emotional pull of a ballad, or the rhythm of hip-hop, each genre serves a purpose in different moments of my life. Music gives me peace, helps me focus, and sometimes even says what I can’t put into words.

It’s always there — in quiet nights, long walks, or just chilling in my room. That’s why I value it so much. It’s not just something I listen to, it’s something I feel.
