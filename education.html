My Academic Journey

My academic journey began at the age of four when I was enrolled in Tadika Umi, a local kindergarten in my neighborhood. Those early years were my first exposure to learning, reading, and socializing with other children. From there, I moved on to primary school at SK Pauh Jaya, where I built my foundational knowledge and developed a strong interest in general knowledge.

For lower secondary, I was selected to attend MRSM Transkrian — a boarding school that provided me with exposure to a more competitive academic environment and independent living. It was here that I learned to balance academics with responsibilities and self-discipline.

After completing PT3, I transferred to SMK Pauh Jaya to complete my upper secondary education. I sat for SPM in 2020 and later continued my studies at UiTM Kedah. I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Library Informatics, driven by my interest in knowledge management and research.

📚 Future Academic Goals

I aspire to continue my education by pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Library and Information Management or Knowledge Management. In the long term, I aim to deepen my expertise in digital archiving, metadata systems, and information architecture. I also have an interest in becoming an educator, lecturer, or researcher who can contribute to the development of future librarians and information professionals.

However, I am in dilemma whether i should pursue my dream in getting a Bachelor's Degree in History considering my interest in history and research. Guess I'll follow the flow and go whenever life takes me...

Regardless, I still believe that academic growth should be aligned with personal values and real-world impact. I plan to stay updated with evolving digital library technologies, advance my cataloguing and metadata skills, and explore research opportunities in historical documentation and digital humanities in the future.
